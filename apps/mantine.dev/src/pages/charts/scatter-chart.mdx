## Usage

`ScatterChart` plots the relationship between two numeric values. Set `xAxisKey` and `yAxisKey` to select fields from the data.

<Demo data={ChartsDemos.scatterChart} />

## Multiple series

Provide multiple series with distinct names and colors to compare groups. Axis, tooltip, legend, grid, and value-formatting props match cartesian charts.
