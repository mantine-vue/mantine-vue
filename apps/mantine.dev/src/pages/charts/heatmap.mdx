## Usage

`Heatmap` maps date keys in `YYYY-MM-DD` format to numeric intensity values.

<Demo data={ChartsDemos.heatmap} />

## Date range and colors

Use `startDate`, `endDate`, and `domain` to control the displayed interval and scale. Provide `colors` to customize intensity levels.
