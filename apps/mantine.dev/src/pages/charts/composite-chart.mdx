## Usage

`CompositeChart` combines bar, line, and area series in one coordinate system. Set `type` on each series item.

<Demo data={ChartsDemos.compositeChart} />

## Series options

Use `lineProps`, `areaProps`, and `barProps` for global or per-series ECharts customization. Common axis, tooltip, legend, and reference-line props match the other cartesian charts.
